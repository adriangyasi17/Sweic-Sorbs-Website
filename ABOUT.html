SWEIC & SORBS IS . . . . . .


........a movement founded in 2022 by Keith, King and Adrian.
We all grew up in London and then went on to study at universities in the UK and Germany.
With Adrian’s biological background alongside Keith and King's specialisation in business administration and entrepreneurship,
our combined skills and experience resulted in the perfect match for this exciting venture.
King and Adrian now live in Düsseldorf, where they manage our EU logistics, while Keith is in charge of our operations in London.
Our passion for natural skincare started in early 2017 with our first experimentations.
We instantly fell in love with the science behind every formulation and the challenges of cold process soap and cream production.
Since then we have immersed ourselves in the world of botany as a means to harness the wonderful properties of herbs and natural oils and butters for our products.
In 2021 we decided to quit our jobs and moved forward with founding Sweich & Sorbs, which specialises in handmade, natural, vegan and environmentally friendly cosmetics,
which are sold in entirely bio-degradable packaging.
All ingredients used in our products are sourced from Keith and Adrian’s father’s farm in the lush hills of the Eastern Region in Ghana.
Shea, Coconut, Almond and Cocoa are the 4 ingredients that form the base of all our products and we always make sure that our source materials are never processed
so they are delivered to you in their virgin state with all their minerals intact.
At Sweic & Sorbs we find new ways of contributing towards a healthier and more sustainable future and this gives us great motivation.
We really appreciate the love and support we have received so far, and we hope to continue our journey with you.
With vybz, more life and blessings,
Keith, King & Adrian


Our purpose is to sustainably produce body care products completely naturally and waste free.
